Cornbread

Description

Whatever you were expecting, it's not that. It's just canned corn dumped on top of wonderbread

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Wonder Bread
  • 1 Can of corn

Steps

  1. Place Wonder Bread on plates.
  2. Open can of corn, strain, and rinse any preservatives.
  3. Dump corn on top of Wonder Bread.
