Cornbread

Description

The most unexpected of dishes. When you tell them they're getting cornbread and then serve them this you'll subvert all their expectations!

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of corn (any brand)
  • 2 Wonder Bread slices

Steps

  1. Place both slices of Wonder Bread on a plate.
  2. Open can of corn, strain it, and rinse any preservatives off using tap water.
  3. Dump the corn onto both slices of bread. Even coverage is not a concern.
