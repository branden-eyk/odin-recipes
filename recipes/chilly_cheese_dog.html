Chilly Cheese Dog

Description

Favorite food of internationally renowned hero and food connoisseur Sanic the Hedgehog. One bite of this dog will have you saying "Cumon step it up!!!!!"

Ingredients

  • 1 tube steak, hereafter referred to as "weiner"
  • 1 Wonder Bread brand hot dog bun
  • 1 American cheese-like substance
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place hot dog bun on plate in an opened position
  2. Place American cheese-like substance on bread
  3. Place weiner on American cheese-like substance
  4. Flank weiner with two ice cubes on either side
  5. Consume
